Pack & Save

Pack & Save is a bundle of our most popular services which has already proven itself to be a great fit for many of our customers. Pack & Save includes: seat selection (excluding extra legroom seats), a 20 kg checked-in bag (149 x 119 x 171 cm), plus one (+1) carry-on bag (40x30x20 cm), which must be placed under the seat, and online check-in up to 30 days before flight departure. 20 kg checked-in bag. All in & Full flex

All in & Full flex is for those who like to be prepared for everything and like to enjoy the comfort of our full service. It includes a large amount of extra services at a discount price. It provides great flexibility and offers the most comfortable passenger experience. All in & Full flex includes premium seat selection with the extra legroom seats, along with feature which allows you to change your flight date if needed, without any extra charge. You can also enjoy our Flex Priority service, which lets you take an extra trolley bag (of max. size 55 x 40 x 23cm, 10 kg), along with a free carry-on bag (max. 40 x 30 x 20 cm). It is guaranteed you can bring both bags with you into the cabin area (unless it is impossible due to operational restrictions), and you will be called first for boarding. In addition, you can bring a 32 kg checked-in bag (max. size 149 x 119 x 171 cm). You will also receive flight Information updates and have the option of choosing online check-in 30 days before your flight departure or Airport check-in.
